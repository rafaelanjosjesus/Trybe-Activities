Let's reproduce this item scheme in your browser! Copy the codes below and paste them into your VS Code, creating a file index.htmland a style.css.

You probably don't know some of the properties used in this code, but don't worry, for now focus on the classes that you should change according to the instructions in the comments that are inside the style.css file .

index.html

Copy
<!DOCTYPE html>

  <div class="caixa width-and-height" style="background: #036b52">A</div>
  <div class="caixa width-and-height padding" style="background: #41197f;">B</div>

  <div class="caixa width-and-height padding margin border" style="background: #3898EC">D</div>
</body>
</html>
